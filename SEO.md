SEO checklist
=============

- Ensure `title` and `meta description` are set per page (root has defaults).
- Add `og:image` meta pointing to a social preview image.
- Ensure `public/sitemap.xml` is up-to-date and referenced in `robots.txt`.
- Add structured data (JSON-LD) for local business on contact/about pages.
- Verify server-side rendering of critical meta for crawlers (Vercel handles prerendering).
